Welcome to Africa Hotel
Gebre Guracha

Africa Hotel is located just 3hrs from Bole International Airport and is centrally located in Gebre Guracha town on larger roads to Bahir Dar, connecting to the large artery roads of Addis Ababa. The hotel stands within a short walking distance to the from the main road just on the asphalt and within a 2-minute when you get off train. The National Bank of Ethiopia, government offices and business offices are also within short distance of the hotel.
